1. Information We Collect

Shopping List does not collect personal data or usage analytics on developer-controlled servers.

No personal data collection declared: Shopping List does not collect, store, or transmit personal information, account details, analytics identifiers, or location data to the developer.

2. How We Use Information

Any list content you create in Shopping List is used only to provide local app functionality. We do not use your personal information for ad targeting or profiling.

3. Permissions

Shopping List may request only minimal permissions required for core features. Permissions can be reviewed or revoked from device settings at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Shopping List does not include third-party advertising or tracking SDKs for developer data collection. Platform providers like Google Play may process data under their own policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties because we do not collect personal data on our servers.

6. Data Retention

Since we do not maintain a central user database for Shopping List, there is no developer-side retention of personal user information.

7. Children Privacy

Shopping List does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Contact us if you believe information was provided in error.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You can uninstall the app or revoke permissions at any time. Depending on your jurisdiction, additional privacy rights may apply.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date will be revised whenever updates are published.
