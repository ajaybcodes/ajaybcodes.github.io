Tables 1-20: Listen & Speak Privacy Policy

This policy describes how Codevity Apps handles information when you use Tables 1-20: Listen & Speak. The app is designed for children and learning-focused use.

Information We Collect

  • No personal data collected: We do not collect, store, or share personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details.
  • Voice recognition: The app may use your device microphone for learning features. Voice data is processed locally on the device and is not stored or transmitted to developer servers.
  • Usage data: We may receive anonymous non-personal usage information (for example, crash diagnostics) through platform-level systems to improve stability.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve app features such as listening, speaking, and practice modes.
  • To analyze app performance and resolve technical issues.

Children Privacy

This app is designed for children and does not collect personal information. Parents and guardians can use the app with confidence that child privacy is respected.

Third-Party Services

We do not include third-party advertising or tracking SDKs for developer-side personal data collection. Distribution platforms such as Google Play may process technical data under their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
