Privacy Policy

Tables 1-20: Listen & Speak is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • No Personal Data Collected: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email, or contact details.
  • Voice Recognition: The app uses your device’s microphone for voice recognition features. Voice data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, non-personal usage statistics (such as feature usage and crash reports) to improve app performance and user experience. This data does not identify you.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve app features, such as listening, speaking, and practice modes.
  • To analyze app performance and fix issues.

Children’s Privacy

This app is designed for children and does not collect personal information. Parents can use the app with confidence that their child’s privacy is protected.

Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services that collect personal data. If any third-party services are added in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Changes to This Policy
